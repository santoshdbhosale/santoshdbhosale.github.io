A pharmacist-turned-proteomics scientist with a PhD and 15+ years of experience spanning wet-lab mass spectrometry, computational proteomics, and — most recently — AI/ML for drug discovery. I bridge deep domain biology with scalable data pipelines.

What I Do

🔬 Proteomics Research

Mass spectrometry-based proteomics (DIA/DDA), PTM enrichment (phospho, glyco, cysteine), biomarker discovery from serum/plasma, label-free & isobaric quantification (TMT/iTRAQ).

🤖 AI/ML for Drug Discovery

LLM-based data extraction (Llama, Gemini) from ClinicalTrials.gov & PubChem, Python automation for biocuration, ML pipeline development, agile collaboration with data engineers.

📦 Product & Project Management

End-to-end delivery of omics projects (SOW to deliverable), cross-functional team leadership, biomarker module product ownership, stakeholder and vendor management.
