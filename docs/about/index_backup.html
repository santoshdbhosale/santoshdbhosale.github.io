<h2>Career Journey</h2>
<p>My career represents a unique evolution from bench scientist to AI-driven drug discovery leader. Starting with hands-on proteomics research during my PhD and postdoc, I developed deep expertise in mass spectrometry, biomarker discovery, and computational analysis.</p>

<p>At Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, I translated this research expertise into clinical applications, developing new platforms and improving laboratory workflows.</p>

<p>Today, I leverage this scientific foundation to lead biomarker module development, integrating cutting-edge AI/ML technologies with proteomics knowledge to accelerate drug discovery. This journey has equipped me with a rare combination of deep scientific expertise and practical AI/ML implementation skills, positioning me at the forefront of computational biology and pharmaceutical innovation.</p>
